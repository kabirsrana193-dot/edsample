Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What is the batch size? +
We keep batches small — a maximum of 15–20 students for school classes and up to 20–25 for competitive exam batches. This ensures every student gets personal attention.
Is a free demo class available? +
Yes! Every new student is welcome to attend a free 1-hour demo class in any subject before enrolling. We want you to feel confident before you commit.
Do you offer online classes? +
Yes, we offer both in-person and live online classes via Google Meet / Zoom. Study material is shared digitally for online students.
What boards do you cover? +
We cover CBSE, ICSE, and major state boards. Please mention your board at the time of enquiry so we can assign the right faculty and material.
Are there any scholarships or fee concessions? +
Yes — we offer merit-based scholarships for high-performing students and sibling discounts. Ask our counsellor for details during your enquiry call.
How are parents kept informed? +
Monthly report cards, WhatsApp group updates, and parent-teacher meetings every quarter ensure you're always up to date on your child's progress.
